How to Play
1. Wait for the orbital planets to perfectly align with the next tile in the path.
2. Tap `SPACE` exactly on the beat to swap the pivot point and lock onto the new tile.
3. 90-degree turns take exactly half a beat. Be ready to double-tap!


Level Editor
From the main menu, press and **hold** the `SPACE` bar for 1.5 seconds to unlock the Level Architect.
* Use `W`, `A`, `S`, `D` to draw a track.
* Press `Z` to undo your last tile.
* Click Save & Play to instantly test your creation.
